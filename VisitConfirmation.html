Please park your vehicle in our Visitor Parking Lot (Lot 14), located at 1144 East Monroe St, Springfield, MO 65807 (Monroe Street is also known as Bear Boulevard on the Missouri State Campus). Instructions on registering your vehicle will be sent closer to your visit date. 

Tips for a successful visit

  • View our Visitors Guide for helpful information.
  • Give yourself some extra time to make it to campus.
  • Wear comfortable shoes!
  • Be sure to dress for the weather and consider bringing an umbrella.
  • Retain this message for reference on the day of your visit.
  • If the university shuts down due to dangerous weather conditions, visits will be canceled. Check www.missouristate.edu for cancellation notices.

Need help?

If you have questions or need to reschedule your visit, please call the Campus Visit Desk at 417-836-6614 or email CampusVisitDesk@MissouriState.edu. The Campus Visit Desk is open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Central Time, Monday through Friday, during days when the university is open. If you have questions on the day of your visit, call our Welcome Center Front Desk at 417-836-8275.
