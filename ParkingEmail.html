Parking instructions

Please park your vehicle in our Visitor Parking Lot (Lot 14), located at 1144 East Monroe St, Springfield, MO 65807 (Monroe Street is also known as Bear Boulevard on the Missouri State Campus). The entrance to the Visitor Parking Lot is located south of the intersection of Monroe Street (Bear Boulevard) and South Florence Avenue. After parking your vehicle, please note your license plate number and proceed to the northwest entrance to the Davis-Harrington Welcome Center where you will find an electronic parking meter.

Register your vehicle

Follow these instructions to register your vehicle for your visit:

  1. Press any key on the electronic parking meter to begin.
  2. Input your vehicle license plate number. Please press keys firmly and double check that your full license plate number has been entered.
  3. When prompted to enter the number of hours you will be parking, input 9 hours.
  4. Indicate that you have a coupon code.
  5. Input the following coupon code: {{Parking Code}}, which will allow you to park for free.

If there are no parking spaces available in the Visitor Parking Lot, pull up to the entrance to the Welcome Center and go inside to inform the front desk. A staff member will issue you a paper pass and give you instructions on where to find an alternative lot in which to park. Directions to campus and other map resources are located at map.missouristate.edu.

Check in

Once you have registered your vehicle, proceed to the Davis-Harrington Welcome Center and check in for your visit at the front desk. We will note your license plate number and get your oriented for your visit.

Need help?

If you have questions or need to reschedule your visit, please call the Campus Visit Desk at 417-836-6614 or email CampusVisitDesk@MissouriState.edu. The Campus Visit Desk is open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Central Time, Monday through Friday, during days when the university is open. If you have questions on the day of your visit, call our Welcome Center Front Desk at 417-836-8275.

We look forward to welcoming you to Missouri State University!
